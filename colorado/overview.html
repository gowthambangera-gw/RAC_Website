Overview

The Colorado RAC Program is a federally mandated initiative under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, designed to identify and recover improper Medicaid payments. HMS has served as the RAC vendor since [year], partnering with [department name] to ensure program integrity and financial stewardship. This program supports the state’s mission to reduce improper payments, enhance program integrity, and provide education and resources to Medicaid providers.

As the state RAC, HMS is authorized to conduct [ post-payment reviews] of claims submitted for [fee-for-service (FFS)]. The program is designed to identify underpayments and overpayments, and to assist [department name] in recovering improper payments made to providers under Medicaid.

Scope of Work

Complex Reviews

Targeted audits requiring medical record review, conducted by clinicians and coders.

Automated Reviews

Data-driven audits that identify improper payments without requiring medical records.

Population Covered

Currently focused on Fee-for-Service (FFS) Medicaid claims.
